The Minds Behind IPFS

Our Leadership

Founder & CEO

Mr. Pawan Ghavri

Mr. Pawan Ghavri has a degree in B. Com Honours from University of Delhi (Class of 1994) and completed his Post Graduation in Sales & Marketing from YMCA in 2000.

He started his journey in Wealth management in the year 1999 with Bajaj Capital Ltd, where he performed various roles from being a Trainee to Branch Manager to Business Development Manager (Delhi), and finally Senior Manager PCG Wealth Management Services till April 2005.

He also worked with ABN Amro Bank NV as Van Gogh Preferred Banking Relationship Manager till November 2005. After gaining this rich institutional experience, he took up a prestigious assignment at Dawnaday AV, Private Wealth Ltd (U.K) as Senior Relationship Manager HNI, before starting his entrepreneurship journey. Invest & Prosper Financial Services (IPFS) was established in 2007.

In these 17 years, he has won collective confidence and trust through his vast 25+ years of experience. His first and foremost motto of forming IPFS was to ensure that clients are able to create wealth through suitable investment options matching their future goals. At Invest and Prosper, the Client’s interest always comes first.

Chief Operating Officer

Ms. Ritu Ghavri

Ms. Ritu Ghavri graduated from Lady Irwin College in 1997 and completed her Masters in International Business in 1999.

She began her corporate journey in International Logistics in 1999 and excelled in this field until 2009. In 2009, she joined IPFS to lead our efforts in empowering women to achieve financial independence.

Since then, she has been working relentlessly towards spreading financial awareness. She believes financial literacy is as important for women as any other household responsibility, especially given their longer life spans and the need to plan for education, children's milestones, and retirement.

Thanks to Ms. Ritu’s great efforts, IPFS is witnessing more women getting involved in financial planning for their families. She also oversees all **Operations** at Invest and Prosper Financial Services.
